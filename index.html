About us






RobôCIn is a research group of the Infomatics Center at UFPE, which resolve problems using robotics. The group was created in 2015 by 12 students and the professors Edna Barros and Hansenclever Bassani.Today we count with 36 students and 4 the professors to coordinate the team to reach innovative solutions using A.I., computer vision, mechanics and electronics combined in robots.
